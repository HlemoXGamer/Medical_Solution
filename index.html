Welcome to Medsol

Medsol was founded in 2014 as a laboratory equipment supplier with a focused portfolio focused on genetics and molecular biology with HQ in Cairo, Egypt. Medsol created its mission on providing innovative solutions to the served markets, helping the healthcare industry to overcome challenges in diagnostic and research areas. As our journey moves forward, Medsol has expanded its presence in other markets such as KSA, UAE and our newest branch in Morocco. Our expert team alongside the unique Portfolio guarantee makes technology more user-friendly and supports diagnostic confidence for all. Medsol’s traditional business model focuses on delivering quality and reliable products to exceed expectations.
